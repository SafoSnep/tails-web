Download and Install

Please don't go looking for Tails in the App Store – its rules deny an app like Tails point-blank. Instead, the suggested installation method is to “sideload” the app via AltStore. No jailbreak required!

Install with AltStoreAltStore Source

Requires iOS 14 or iOS 15. You may also download Version 2.1 as .ipa (2.14 MB, ) for a manual installation.

SHA-256: 31d36e5b6a585db26de0c5b4f4485762a227111a38df1ba97ccfba0b1fd987e3

Please read the FAQ page first if you're unfamiliar with AltStore or need help installing it, and for information on alternative installation methods.

Drag-and-Drop

Tails supports iPadOS's Drag-and-Drop system with multiple items, and multitasking with Split Screen and Slide Over.

It works great with 3rd party apps that also support Drag-and-Drop, and for saving multiple images to your device or any cloud service in your Files app by dragging them over.

(Alternatively, save single images to your device by long pressing on them, for example on devices without drag-and-drop support.)

Expanded Layout

On iPad, a lot more thumbnails are shown per screen, with even more thumbnails available as an optional layout enabled in the app's settings.
